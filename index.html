Pharaonic Symbols

  • The deck contains 4 pairs of cards with healthy food, and all the others are with unhealthy meals.
  • The cards are arranged randomly on the grid with the food face down.
  • The gameplay rules are very simple: flip over two hidden cards at a time to locate the ones that match!
